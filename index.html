Delete Your Account

If you wish to delete your account from the ALKALIVE application, please follow the steps below carefully:

  1. Open the ALKALIVE app on your device.
  2. Navigate to My Account > Settings > Manage Account.
  3. Read the full description about deleting your account. Make sure you fully understand the consequences before proceeding.
  4. If you agree to continue, click on Delete Account and follow the on-screen instructions to complete the process.

Note: Deleting your account is an irreversible action. All your data will be permanently removed.
